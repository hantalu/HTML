Friend System

Inviting Friends

Inviting Friends

• Players can Login with Facebook and use their facebook account to invite their friends to join them in Poker game
• For each successful invitation, players will get free chips and they can send 50 invitation at max / day

Successful Invitation

• Players (A) will get an amount of free chips for each invitation sent
• Each invitation will send a "Buddies Code" to their friend (easy to remember)
• When a new player logging in the game (with Facebook) for the 1st time, they will be asked for using Buddies Code
• New user can skip "Buddies Code" feature or input a Buddies Code sent by their friend to receive a starter gift pack
• Once a Buddies Code entered, the Player (A) who sent the Buddies Code will receive an extra reward

Daily Friend Gift

Daily Friend Gift

• Players can send Free Gift to their friends each day
• Each Friend Gift pack contains an amount of Chips and a Random Support item (update later)
• Players can only send Friend Gift to a friend once a day

Inviting Friends to join room

Inviting Friends to join room

• Players can invite their friends to join with them when they are in a table

Friend Relationship

Friend Relationship

• By Sending Daily Friend Gifts, Inviting Friend to join room, v.v... Players can increase Friend Relation Ship level with a friend
• The higher the Relationship level, the faster the "Momentum" Level up speed when Players play with their friend in tables
• The higher the Relationship level, the more Chips and better reward sent for friends each day

Momentum

Momentum

• Depends on the amount of cash paid for each game, "Momentum" level of a player will be increased
• The higher level of the Momentum, the faster EXP farming speed
• After a game, player will get EXP point, the higher level of the Momentum, the more EXP point earned
• By incresing level, players can get Level Gift pack

Rank

Rank

• Player can be ranked by their WEEKLY TOTAL Winning Cash in every games
• Player can see their rank in Rank Table
• The higher the Rank, the better Rank Gift player can receive in the weekend
