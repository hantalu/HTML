0. Main Game

0. Main Game

• 1 Roulette Bet: = Line bet
• Total bet: = Line bet + 4 x Roulette bet
• Win point of Outer Roulette: Roulette bet x Roulette ODD
• Win point of Inner Spin: Line bet x Spin ODD
• Total win point: Roulette Win + Spin Win

Spin ODD

• How to play?
  • After a spin round, player will get a Spin Win if there are 3 or 4 same symbols on the Inner Spin
  • Spin win = Line bet x Spin ODD
  • Just take a look at the example above, SPIN ODD of 3 Owls is 5, so the player win Spin Win = Line Bet (0.2) x Spin Odd (5) = 1
  • Spin Win works individually to the Outer Roulette, which means no matter the result of the Outer Roulette, Spin Win will be paid if there are 3 or more same symbols appear in the Inner Spin

Roulette ODD

• How to play?
  • After a spin round, player will get a Roulette Win if the Outer Roulette stop at 1 same symbol at least to the symbol(s) in the Inner Spin
  • Roulette win = Roulette bet x Roulette ODD
  • Just take a look at the example above, ROULETTE ODD of Owl is x2, so the player win Roulette Win = Roulette Bet (0.2) x Roulette Odd (2) = 0.4
  • In the case there are more than 1 symbols matched in the Inner Spin, the Total Roulette Win will be the Total of ALL ROULETTE WIN (Sum up)

1. Extra Spin

1. Extra Spin

• After a spin, no matter the players get a Win or not, they can play the Extra Spin
• With Extra Spin, player will pay an exact same amount of Total Bet like it was in the Normal Spin
• The spin will start again like it was in the Normal Spin
• With an Extra Spin, HOLD feature will be triggered
• Extra spin CANNOT be played after a normal spin if Bet was changed

2. HOLD in Extra Spin

• HOLD feature can only be trigger after a Normal Spin with some special conditions:

Case 1: Nothing to HOLD

HOLD will not be suggested in the case 4 symbols in the Inner Spin are diffrent to each other and also different to the Outer Roulette

Case 2: Promissing Win in Extra Spin

Althought player didn't get any WIN in this round, he was suggested to HOLD 2 Tazan symbols
Coz the Extra Spin can trigger BIG ROULETTE WIN (if the result of next Roulette is Tazan) or BIG SPIN WIN (if there are 1 Tazan symbol at least appear in the Inner Spin in the Extra Spin)
So, HOLD will be suggested in these cases:
  • Get Spin Win or Roulette Win
  • There are 2 same symbols at least in the Inner Spin with the ODDS higher than WOLF (no matter the result of Outer Roulette)

  • There are 2 same Monkey symbols at least in the Inner Spin(no matter the result of Outer Roulette) and 2 other symbols are different from Monkey symbols and diffrent to each other

3. Extra Spotlight

6. Complex Symbol

6. Complex Symbol

• By activating Complex Symbol feature, player can increase the chance to win more point
• By inscreasing Bet, 4 symbols at the 4 corners of the game screen will changed to Complex Symbol
• With Bet >= 10% of Max Bet, the symbol at the Uper left corner will changed to Complex.
• With Bet >= 40% of Max Bet, 1 more symbol at the Uper right corner will changed to Complex.
• With Bet >= 60% of Max Bet, last 2 symbol at the Lower left / right corner will changed to Complex.
• Each Complex symbol is the combination of the 2 symbols which can help player to earn more win point. Once the Outer Roulette stops at a Complex symbol, all 2 symbols in the Complex combination are counted for Roulette Win or Spin Win if existed

7. Bonus Game

7. Bonus Game

• Once the result of the Inner Spin has 3 Monkey Symbol at least (any position) and TOTAL BET is >= 20% MAX TOTAL BET, Bonus game can be triggered
• The Bonus Counter will be stopped immediately and this is the Win point availabled for the Bonus game
• After pressing Start button, Bonus Monkey Symbols in the game screen will be lighten up consecutively.
• Player can press Stop button at anytime to stop the running light, in the case the light stops at the Big Monkey Symbol, player will win BONUS WIN POINT

Bet more to win more bonus

• Once Total Bet >= 20% Max Total bet: Players can win 20% of Bonus Counter
• Once Total Bet >= 40% Max Total bet: Players can win 40% of Bonus Counter
• Once Total Bet >= 60% Max Total bet: Players can win 100% of Bonus Counter

Bonus Counter

• Bonus Counter in server side will be randomly kept changing
• Once entering the game, Bonus Counter will be randomly kept changing (value can be different from server, It doesn't matter but the random range must be the same)
• The Counter will be stopped (get value from server) once the Spin round started
• While waitting for Extra Spin, the Counter will be freezed
• In the case players don't play Extra Spin and press Spin to start another spin round, the Bonus Counter will be updated to another value (get from server)
• The Counter will be kept changing again once and only players press Take button to completely end a Spin Round

10. Buttons's Status

10. Buttons's Status

• 1st opening the game, all buttons at Bottom Bar will be enabled except Extra Button (4 HOLD options will not appear)
• While spinning normaly, Bet - Max Bet - Extra Button will be disabled and Spin button will change to Stop button
• After a normal spin, all buttons will be enabled (4 HOLD options will appear at disabled state and which one is suggested will be enabled)
• In Extra Spin, all DISABLED HOLD OPTIONS will change to normal Hold option in disabled state (no coin icon)
• After Extra Spins, Bet - Max bet - Extra button will be disabled and Spin Button will change to Take button in enabled state
• After Extra Spins, all 4 HOLD OPTIONS remained the state like they were before the Extra Spin and can not be pressed anymore
• After Extra Spins and win NOTHING, Main game will be reset and all 4 Hold Options will be turned off
• BET and TAKE button will reset Main Game and whole 4 Hold Options (make them disappeared)
• Spin button will turn off all 4 HOLD OPTIONS to start a new Normal Spin

11. Balance Updated Rule

11. Balance Updated Rule

• Balance will only be updated once TAKE button is pressed
• After a Normal Spin, Total Win (if existed) will be kept by server. If Game Client is ended at this moment, the Total Win will be kept in server until players re-log into the game and press TAKE button
• For every Extra Spin, Total Win will be removed, the new result will be re-counted after Extra Spin (Which means the final result if the result of Extra Spin)
• Players cannot withdraw the Total Win while they are waitting for Extra Spin
• For a spin that trigger Bonus Game, Total Win will not be shown after the spin but after finishing playing Bonus Game

12. Replay Rule

12. Replay Rule

• After a normal spin, if players don't play the Extra spin yet, they will see the exact same game screen as before the game client was killed. Hold Suggestion must be shown kept too
• In the case result of Normal spin was not shown yet and the game client was killed, the result will be shown after players re-log into the game and wait for Extra Spin + Hold Suggestion (if existed)
• In the case result of Extra spin was not shown yet and the game client was killed, the result will be shown after players re-log into the game
• While waitting for Extra Spin, if players turn off the Hold Suggestion, the Hold Suggestion must be re-shown after players re-log into the game
